Dhanush (born Venkatesh Prabhu Kasthuri Raja on July 28, 1983) is a versatile Indian actor, director, producer, lyricist, and playback singer primarily working in Tamil cinema. He debuted in 2002 with Thulluvadho Ilamai, directed by his father Kasthuri Raja. Known for his acclaimed performances, he won a National Film Award for Best Actor for Aadukalam in 2011 and received international recognition for the song "Why This Kolaveri Di" in the same year
